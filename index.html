Youtube 3D in LEAP Motion


Here we developed a 3D version of YouTube using THREE.js and added gesture support using LEAP.js and LEAP controller. We implemented gesture based scrolling , play , pause , mute , unmute and quality change. It has intuitive and attractive 3D user interface and also boasts a little 3D animation.

Contribute to our project by forking our repository: https://github.com/web-LEAP/youtube3D
